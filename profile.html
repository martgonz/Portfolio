About Me- Martha Gonzalez

I've lived in several towns and cities in the West. I came to Seattle as a temporary resident with intentions of staying for seven months. Two years later I'm still here. I decided to attend the evening class with Epicodus because I wanted to find my creativity again. I feel that over the years of maintaining certain jobs I've lost the opportunity to be creative and I'm struggling to find that intrinsic reward. I needed an intervention in my life.
I decided to expand my knowledge and learn to code mostly for the challenge and the opportunity to regain the creative person I used to be. I know life is very short and it gains speed every year, I do not want to look back and say "I wish I would have" my biggest fear is holding my self back, to live paralyzed. My philosophy is to try something new on a regular basis because I might discover a new passion.
Regardless of where this course leads me I am very excited to take the initial step of exploring. At this time I have to admit the class has been something I look forward to doing and I've enjoyed meeting my classmates.
